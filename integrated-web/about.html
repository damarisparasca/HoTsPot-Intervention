Who are we?

We are final year Computational Social Science students from the University of Amsterdam. This website is a part of our thesis project, conducted in collaboration with organisation Valueing Blue. The goal of our research was to deepen the understanding of WaterCredits as a potential solution to improving water quality in the Netherlands. The detailed rationale and methodology can be found in our report. For questions, feel free to reach out to any of us.
