What do you need to know as a policymaker?

Water contamination isn’t just about reducing pollution, it is about safeguarding resources. Water Credits encourage the best practices, without being too much of a burden. It requires several baseline metrics in order to be implemented. Another consideration is wether revenue should be invested in restoration or treatment of water bodies.


The future of WaterCredits

Emissions prediction model

Explore how pollutant levels in water are expected to change over time. This tool analyses historical trends from national monitoring data to predict future concentrations of key pollutants, such as nitrate.

WaterCredits market model estimator

Optimization approach that sets baselines for emissions, demonstrating wether farmers need to purchase or can sell credits.
