What do you need to know as a policymaker?

Water contamination isn’t just about reducing pollution, it is about safeguarding resources. Water Credits encourage the best practices, without being too much of a burden. It requires several baseline metrics in order to be implemented. Another consideration is wether revenue should be invested in restoration or treatment of water bodies.
