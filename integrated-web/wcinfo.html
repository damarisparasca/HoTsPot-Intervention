How does the calculator work?







The WaterCredits and nitrogen emissions calculator is designed to help for farmers assess their current management and its impact on water quality. It compares the Government's set nitrogen emissions with the farmer's actual emissions and assigns WaterCredits based on this.

Its functionality is based on the INITIATOR model developed by De Vries, Kros, Oenema, and De Klein's (2003).
