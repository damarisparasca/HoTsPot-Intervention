What do you need to know as a farmer?

Sustainability in farming doesn’t need to financially burden you. Dutch farmers are already adopting practices like precision fertilizer, manure storage systems, and grazing strategies. These techniques allow farmers to cut down on their nutrient runoff and improve surface water.

WaterCredits

WaterCredits is a system to benefit those who decide to become more sustainable whilst allowing those who choose to remain to have the ability to do so.

WaterScore

Water score is a simple metric that rates farmers overall water use. It is based on several indicators and will determine how sustainable farmers are, hence how many credits need to be purchased.
