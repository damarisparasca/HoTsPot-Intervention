How do WaterCredits works?

Water Credits operate by farms or industries that generate fewer pollutants than a set baseline can earn “credits.” Those who exceed their pollution limits must buy these credits to offset their extra emissions. This creates both an incentive for cleaner practices and a funding stream for additional water protection measures. Policymakers play a key role in setting fair baselines and overseeing credit transactions, ensuring transparency and tangible environmental benefits. By rewarding responsible water usage and pollution reduction, Water Credits help align economic interests with ecological well-being.
