About Us

The Problem

Water sustains all forms of life, but the globe is going through a water crisis. 2.2bn people worldwide lack safe drinking water, and this figure is set to increase. The Netherlands is historically a water-rich country, but recent data shows a decline in water quality. The agriculture industry tends to be the root cause, namely livestock farming, due to excess manure leading to nutrient runoff. Despite EU mandates, pollution is still above a complying threshold.

The Solution: Water Credits

Water Credits comes as a solution to the water crisis. A market-based system to promote sustainable water use and reduce overall pollution. Businesses affected can choose to change their practices, and seek the rewards of a system, or remain the same, pay a tax, which would then be reinvested into water footprint offset programs.
